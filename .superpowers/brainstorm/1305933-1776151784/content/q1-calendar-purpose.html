What's the primary purpose of the calendar?

This shapes everything — layout, data shown, how you interact with it

A

Review & reminder planner

See upcoming next_review dates across all notes. Click a day to see what needs attention. The calendar is a smarter version of the Reminders tab — spatially laid out so you can plan your lab week at a glance.

B

Experiment log & history

See past lab activity — notes plotted by their experiment date. Scroll back through months to see what you did on any given day. A visual history of the lab.

C

Full lab calendar — both past & future

Everything on one calendar. Past experiments shown on the day they happened. Upcoming reviews shown on their due date. One unified view of the lab timeline.
