Audit findings:
- **Navbar overflow on mobile** — 8-9 buttons in a 48px nav on 375px phones, no overflow handling → items cut off. Root cause: `@media(max-width:600px)` forced `min-width:44px` on all `.nav-icon-btn` making each 44px wide, totalling 500px+ on 375px screens.
- **Nav drawer** — fixed `width:300px` breaks on phones narrower than 300px (iPhone SE 1st gen = 320px leaves no room)
- **AIBar grid area** — `const aibarHTML=''` at line 8992 allocated a full-width row in the dashboard grid but rendered nothing — wasted space
- **Alert onclick injection** — `onclick="${a.action}"` in dashboard alerts interpolated raw JS strings. Low-risk (actions are hardcoded) but bad pattern
- **Save button not in finally** — `save-note-btn.disabled=false` was outside try/catch (which is technically correct but not defensive)
- **No Quick Note on dashboard** — existing "📝 Quick Note" button navigated away; no inline widget
