**Root cause: navTo() never opens the drawer**

The nav drawer starts closed. navTo(sec) for list sections (bottles, notes, etc.) only toggles which nd-* panel is active inside the closed drawer — it never calls openNavDrawer(). So every external cross-section link that goes through navTo('bottles') drops you into the bottles section with the drawer closed, which means: no list visible, just the empty state.

This explains your stat card click — you land on sec-bottles with bot-empty showing and the bottle list invisible.

**The second compounding issue: closeNavDrawer() after every save**

Line 12503 (saveBottleAction batch path): renderBottleList(); closeNavDrawer(); — drawer closes after batch save. Then _showSavedQRGrid() renders QR cards in bot-detail. You click a QR → openBottle(id) → bot-detail fills with the batch detail including "View all X bottles in this batch." But drawer is closed.
